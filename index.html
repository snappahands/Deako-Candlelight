Current Reality

Six products. Multiple vendors.
One below average experience

Safe Streets installs and connects a mix of third-party hardware — Genie, Nest, Kwikset and Google — through Google Home. It works, but installation, service, support, and follow-up are inconsistent and not meeting expectations. With no reliable point of contact, the Candlelight and homebuyer experience is suffering. Vivint replaces the patchwork with a vertically integrated system — it builds its own thermostat, doorbell camera, and hub, and Candlelight retains a reliable point of contact in Eric for program management and escalations.
